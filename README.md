🚨 4. JIT ALERTS — The "Never Go Out of Stock" System
JIT = Just-In-Time. This is the most powerful feature for high-volume shops.

How it works:

A customer comes to buy "Chain Sprocket 34T". You add it to the bill.
The software detects that the last piece is being sold right now.
Instantly shows a message: "Stock is 0 after this sale — Draft a Purchase Order?"
You click YES. The order is drafted automatically in the background.
You continue billing the customer — no delay, no interruption.
Later, open the Drafts list, confirm the order, and it's ready to send to your supplier.
What you get on your phone (Telegram):

💰 MRP: ₹353.00 | Margin: 28%
📍 Location: Rack A / Col 3
🚗 Fits: Hero Splendor
🏭 Vendor: ABC Auto Parts
Result: You will never run out of a part while billing a customer again. The software manages restocking for you in the background while you focus on the customer.
